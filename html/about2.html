Chairman's Address

The Guangzhou Institute of the Greater Bay Area was established to adapt to the current development of domestic and international situations. The Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area (GBA) has always been at the forefront of China's reform and opening up. The world today is undergoing major changes unseen in a century, and the GBA will play a leading role in achieving China's modernization and common prosperity. GIG must accomplish the following: First, we have to contribute to the development of Guangzhou. Second, we need to promote the integration of Guangzhou with the GBA. Third, we must assist in the process of Hong Kong and Macao integrating into the overall development of mainland China. The successful construction of the GBA will have far-reaching implications for the entire country, thus we have undertaken a huge historical mission. We hope that the GIG will tell the story of Guangzhou and the GBA, building up to the nation’s soft power. In the future, GIG will embark on a path of long-term sustainable development, providing valuable policy recommendations for both government and business decision-makers.
