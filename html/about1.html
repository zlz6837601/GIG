Introduction of GIG

The Guangzhou Institute of the Greater Bay Area (GIG) in Guangzhou is an independent, non-profit research center, established in 2021. Our mission is to promote the development of the Guangdong–Hong Kong–Macao Greater Bay Area (GBA) by providing consulting services for the public and enterprises. We promote scientific, democratic, law-based decision-making for the state and government. GIG is dedicated to becoming the most renowned and influential think tank in the Greater Bay Area by conducting strategic, prospective, and innovative theoretical and policy research.

The establishment of GIG is to deal with current domestic and international developmental problems. The GBA has been the vanguard of China’s economic reform. Under the great changes in today’s world, the GBA endeavors to play an exemplary role in China’s pursuit of modernization and common prosperity. Thus, GIG is the mainstay of providing support to the development of the GBA.

GIG is organized into five research centers: Center for Economic Policy Research, Center for Greater Bay Area and Urban Studies, Center for Corporate Policy Research, Research Center for Artificial Intelligence and Big Data, and Center for Governance and Modernization.

Adhering to the core values of independence, objectivity, and effectiveness, GIG brings together scholars and practitioners with a global vision at home and abroad. We actively respond to major political, economic, and social issues in China and the rest of the world. We are committed to conducting innovative and professional policy research, advocating practical and effective policy alternatives and solutions, and improving the quality of public policies.
