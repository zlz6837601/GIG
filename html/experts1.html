The Council is the decision-making and leadership body of the institute. The Council consists of renowned experts from the mainland, entrepreneurs and experts from Hong Kong and Macao, and entrepreneurs recommended by the initiators of the Council, with the first batch of 17 appointed. Experts serving on the Council are required to have a rigorous scientific spirit, noble professional ethics, high academic research level or rich practical experience, be familiar with the situations in Guangdong, Hong Kong, and Macao, and have a high reputation and influence among peers at home and abroad. They should also be able to ensure their participation in relevant decision-making and consulting activities. The term of office for the Council is three years and can be renewed upon completion of the term.
